About KMCT Hospital

The Kunhitharuvai Memorial Charitable Trust Group (KMCT) Medical College Hospital, located in Manassery Mukkam, Calicut, is a renowned healthcare institution in the Malabar region. It boasts a 700-bed multispeciality hospital that provides comprehensive health care services adhering to global standards. The hospital is well-known for its excellent medical expertise, nursing care, and quality diagnostic services. With a strong commitment to a caring mission and a global vision, KMCT Medical College Hospital aims to improve the health of the community by establishing a standard of excellence in medical education, research, and clinical care.

Our Vision

To be one among the top institutions in the world in every aspect of health care, education, and research.

Our Mission

We strive to provide ethical, cost-effective, and state-of-the-art healthcare services to remedy illness and improve patient care through compassionate healing and advanced research.
